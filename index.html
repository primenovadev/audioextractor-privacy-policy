Privacy Policy

Audio Extractor values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information Collection

Audio Extractor does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or passwords.

2. Media File Access

The app requires access to media files stored on your device in order to convert videos into MP3 audio files. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Advertisements

Audio Extractor may display advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous usage information for personalized and non-personalized ads.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Security

Your files remain on your device. Audio Extractor does not upload your videos or audio files to any server.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected within the application.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer.
